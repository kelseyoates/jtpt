Jay Thebado
Personal Training

JTPT is an independent business located in Stoughton, MA. Jay trains at peoples’ homes and works part-time at Inclusive Fitness in West Roxbury. He is also a corporate fitness consultant.

About Jay

Jay has a bachelor’s degree in nursing from UMass-Lowell and is certified in personal training through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM). He is also a certified Autism Fitness coach. Lastly, Jay is certified in Crisis Prevention Intervention (CPI).

He has coached for the Stoughton Ice Center, Crimson Hockey Club, and Canton High sub-varsity baseball and runs tennis and street hockey clinics with the Stoughton Recreation Department.

Jay is currently training individuals in Stoughton, Sharon, Canton, Dedham, and Franklin.
